Chapter 3 Getting Started in Our Research Group

We’re so thrilled that you’ve decided to join us at our research group at UVA! In this section, we’ll outline some useful tips/steps that will help you get situated within our group specifically. Note that you should keep an eye out on instructions from the Department and UVA!

3.1 6 to 3 Months Before Arriving at UVA

Task Resources/Notes
  • Start looking into housing options for the upcoming term
  • UVA Off Grounds Housing Page: a decent place to look at listings
  • More aggregated resources
  • Confirm logistics for moving to UVA
  • We have some resources to defray a portion of the moving costs for non-local students at the lab. Please check with Fred to see what is available and get things in writing
